Piggy Coin — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Welcome to Piggy Coin. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Piggy Coin is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service:

  • Savings & expense data — All financial entries (amounts, notes, categories) are stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your financial data to any server.
  • Currency preference — Your selected currency is stored locally on your device.
  • Device locale — Used once on first install to suggest a default currency. Not transmitted to any server.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not collect banking or financial account information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Firebase Services

Piggy Coin uses Google Firebase to deliver in-app content updates (Living Updates, Banner Ads, Splash Screen images). Firebase may collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Push notification tokens (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) — used only to deliver app update notifications

This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of notifications in your device settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Content

The app may display banner advertisements and splash screen images hosted on external servers. No personal data is transmitted when loading these images.

5. Data Storage & Security

All your savings and expense records are stored exclusively on your device. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted. We do not maintain backups of your personal financial data.

6. Children's Privacy

Piggy Coin is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
